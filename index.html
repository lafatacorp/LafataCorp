003 -- About

Cam Lafata

Cam Lafata is a sports media professional and entrepreneur based in Dallas-Fort Worth. Born in Dallas and raised partly in Frisco before his family relocated to New England, Cam returned to his hometown to attend Southern Methodist University, where he studies Sport Management through the Simmons School of Education and Human Development alongside business coursework at the Cox School of Business. His concentrations span sports law and ethics, sports data analytics, organizational leadership, and communications.

Cam is an on-air personality at KTCK 96.7/1310 The Ticket, a DFW institution and one of the most successful and influential sports stations in the country, where he contributes live broadcast coverage, editorial commentary, and analysis across all major sports, with specialized expertise in the NHL. That focus is deliberate: his long-term ambition is to work in hockey operations at the professional level.

His academic research examines the behavioral and financial effects of legalized sports betting on young adults, and the rise of prediction markets as an alternative investment vehicle for a younger generation of investors. That second thread became LafataCorp, his venture in predictive markets and sports analytics: a portfolio of trading systems, automated bots, and analytical tools, built and documented openly at lafatacorp.com.
